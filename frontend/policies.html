STORE POLICIES

Shipping, returns and support.

Please read our store policies before placing an order. These policies help us serve customers clearly and professionally.

Shipping Policy

  • Orders are processed after payment confirmation.
  • Delivery time depends on your location and courier availability.
  • Customers must provide the correct delivery address and contact number.
  • Reza Holdings is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect customer details or courier issues outside our control.
  • Local collection or special delivery arrangements may be confirmed through WhatsApp where available.

Returns Policy

  • Due to hygiene and personal care standards, opened or used products cannot be returned.
  • Returns may be accepted only for damaged, incorrect or defective items reported within 48 hours of receiving the order.
  • Customers must provide clear photos/videos of the issue before a return or replacement can be approved.
  • Approved replacements or refunds are handled after inspection and confirmation.

Payment Policy

  • Orders are confirmed only after successful payment.
  • Unpaid orders may be cancelled if payment is not completed.
  • Customers should contact us if they need help completing payment.
